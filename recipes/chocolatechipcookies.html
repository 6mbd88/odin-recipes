Chocolate Chip Cookies

What are chocolate chip cookies?

A classic American dessert and snack, chocolate chip cookies are a sweet cookie made with butter, flour, vanilla, eggs, and plenty of chocolate chips!

There is wide variation in styles of chocolate chip cookies. From thin Tate's style to the uber-thick Levain style to the classic crispy-on-the-outside, chewy-on-the-inside, there is no end to the variations of this versatile treat!

Ingredients

  • 200g butter
  • 450g all purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 250g dark brown sugar
  • 150g white sugar, toasted
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 400g chocolate chips
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp freshly grated nutmeg

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees Farenheit
  2. Cream together butter and sugars until pale and falls from spoon in thick ribbons
  3. Whisk in the eggs one at a time, stirring after each addition until completed incorporated. Stir in vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, salt, and nutmeg
  5. Add the dry ingredients into the wet and stir until just combined
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips, stirring as little as possible
  7. Drop in 1/4 cup rounds onto a greased cookie sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are slightly brown and puffed
  8. Cool on a rack for 10 minutes before moving to a container or plate
